We are Wolverine Robotics! Last year we made it to the semi-finals with our robot, Semi-Seigebreaker. We are proud to represent Hunter High School at the annual FIRST Utah regional.

Our team has been competing in FIRST robotics for about 8 years and we are happy to have this program in our school. We greatly appreciate FIRST putting together these competitions and hosting competitions across the United States!
